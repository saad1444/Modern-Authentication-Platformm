ModernAuth Help Center

Welcome to the ModernAuth Help Center. Whether you’re just getting started or need assistance with advanced features, this guide will help you understand how to make the most out of ModernAuth's secure authentication system.

🔰 Getting Started

To begin using ModernAuth, you need to:

  • Sign up with your email address and create a secure password.
  • Verify your email through the confirmation link.
  • Log in using your credentials to access the dashboard.

🔐 Resetting Your Password

If you forget your password, follow these steps:

  1. Go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password".
  2. Enter your registered email address.
  3. Check your inbox for the reset link and click it.
  4. Enter a new password and confirm.

Tip: Use a strong combination of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.

⚠️ Troubleshooting Login Issues

Facing issues while logging in? Here are common fixes:

  • Ensure that your internet connection is stable.
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies.
  • Double-check your email and password input.
  • If the problem persists, contact our support team.

📱 Enabling Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Two-factor authentication adds an extra layer of security:

  • Navigate to your profile settings.
  • Select “Enable 2FA”.
  • Scan the QR code using Google Authenticator or Authy.
  • Enter the 6-digit code generated by the app.
  • Click “Verify” to complete setup.

🧠 FAQs

Q: How do I contact support?

A: You can reach our support team at support@modernauth.com.
